Butternut Squash Soup

Description

The best butternut squash recipe for a fall night.

Easy to make and very savory.

Yields 6-8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 white onion, peeled and diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 carrots, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 1 Granny Smith apple, cored and roughly chopped
  • 1 medium (~3 to 4 lb) butternut squash, peeled, seeded and diced
  • 1 sprig fresh sage
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne
  • pinch of ground cinnamon and nutmeg
  • ½ cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • fine sea salt and freshly-cracked black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Sauté the onion and garlic.
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large stockpot over medium-high heat. Add the onion and saute for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened and translucent.
    2. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 to 2 more minutes, stirring frequently, until fragrant.
  2. Simmer:
    1. Add the vegetable broth, carrots, apple, butternut squash, sage, cayenne, cinnamon and nutmeg and stir to combine. Continue cooking until the soup reaches a simmer.
    2. Cover, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 20-30 minutes until the vegetables are all tender and mash easily with a fork.
    3. Remove and discard the sage. Stir in the coconut milk.
  3. Purée:
    1. Use an immersion blender to purée the soup until smooth.
  4. Season and serve:
    1. Taste and season the soup generously with salt and pepper as needed.
    2. Serve warm, topped with your desired garnishes, and enjoy!
